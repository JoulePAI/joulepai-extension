Data Consent Required
JoulePAI Wallet needs your permission to access the following data:
  • Current tab URL — to detect Pay with Joules buttons on websites you visit
  • Wallet handle — shared with sites you explicitly connect to
  • Wallet balance — shared with sites you explicitly connect to
Your wallet handle and balance are only shared with websites you connect to. No data is collected by JoulePAI or sent to third parties.
If you decline, the extension cannot function and will offer to uninstall itself.
